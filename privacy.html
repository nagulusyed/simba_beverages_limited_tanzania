Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Simba Beverages Limited. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share with us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it, in plain English.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you explicitly provide to us, or information necessary for the site to function correctly:

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit an enquiry, we collect your name, email address, company name, phone number, and any details you provide about your order.
  • Age Verification Data: We use local storage (a technology similar to cookies but stored entirely on your device) to remember that you have verified you are of legal drinking age. We do not track your specific birthdate, only that you have passed the age gate.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your wholesale enquiries and provide accurate pricing.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders or potential business partnerships.
  • To ensure that you only have to verify your age once per device, improving your browsing experience.

No Third-Party Sales

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is used exclusively by the Simba Beverages sales and customer service teams to fulfill your requests.
